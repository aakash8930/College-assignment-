00. Team AKATUSKI Esports

AKATUSKI Esports is a professional esports team based in India. It was founded in 2023 by MILAN, a young entrepreneur and gamer. The team currently competes in the Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI), Team AKATUSKI is not a part of the BGMI Professional League. AKATUSKI Esports has a roster of 5 players.
